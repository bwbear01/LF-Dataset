Dataset Introduction

We build two real-world paired light field datasets, LF-Raindrop and LF-Dirty, for transparent plane dirt removal tasks. LF-Raindrop contains 88 groups of rainy light field images with manual raindrop occlusion masks. LF-Dirty covers four typical contaminants: raindrops, dust, milk stains and ink marks, with 192 paired light field scenes.

Data Details

All data is captured by Lytro Illum light field camera with 7×7 sub-aperture views. Each group includes degraded dirty light field, clean ground-truth light field and pixel-level annotation mask.
