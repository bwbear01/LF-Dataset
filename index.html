Dataset Introduction & Analysis

The LF-Raindrop dataset is derived from the paper "Depth-aware intra & inter aggregation for light field raindrop removal" and is publicly available for download. The LF-Dirty dataset integrates the LF-Raindrop dataset and will be open to the public in the future. All lfr files in the datasets are accompanied by corresponding white images for decoding.

Data Capture & Format

All data is captured by Lytro Illum light field camera with 7×7 sub-aperture views. Each group includes degraded dirty light field, clean ground-truth light field and pixel-level annotation mask.
